FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior trading experience to get started?

Nope, We don’t leave you to figure it out alone. Support and accountability are built into the program from day one. Whether you're just starting or you've been trading for a while without consistency, we simplify the process. You’ll have access to one-on-one coaching, live sessions, and direct feedback from me and my team.

What makes your trading approach different from others?

I don’t believe in cookie-cutter strategies or endless theory. We focus on actionable techniques grounded in how real markets work, especially how institutions drive price movement. My team and I teach you to recognize those patterns and position yourself accordingly. It’s not just about trading, it’s about thinking like a pro.

Is this long-term investing or active trading?

We’re in the middle of what I call active wealth building. It’s not day trading, but it’s definitely not buy and hold either. We help you shift your capital based on real time market conditions, so you're always aligned with opportunity while minimizing downside.

Do you help with risk management?

Absolutely. Risk management is the foundation of what I teach. We cover how to size your positions properly, set realistic stop levels, and rotate out of risky assets before things turn ugly. You’ll learn how to protect your capital like a pro.
